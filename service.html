Service

Society of Women Engineers: Community Service

As a part of the Community Serivce committee, I met once a week to help plan and manage various service projects. Some of those included: visiting a retirement home and creating no-sew fleece blankets for Project Linus. We also planned for TAMU's Big Event and Kyle Field day.



Society of Women Engineers: Mentorship

After freshman year, I decided to become a mentor to the next class of freshmen and served as a mentor for one year. This was a really good teaching opportunity and helped me become a better leader.



United By Dance

I was the head chairperson of this community service project, which was a fundraiser benefitting the Special Olympics. The show was performed by local dance companies and the ticket and T-shirt sales went to charity. This was a four-month long project that encouraged strong leadership and time-management skills.
